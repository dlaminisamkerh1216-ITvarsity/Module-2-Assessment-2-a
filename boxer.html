BOXER



If there is one store in South Africa that knows exactly how to service their customers, it is Boxer Superstore. This chain store's main aim is to serve the local community by stocking a wide variety of products at low prices. Shoppers love this store so much, that they have named it "Africa's Favourite Discount Supermarket". A combination of their low prices, impeccable customer service and quality products make Boxer Stores incredibly popular. However, it is specifically their prices that make them stand out among other stores.
